About GraphIQ

GraphIQ is a company dedicated to offering digital solutions, including graphic design, custom printing and embossing services, sales of office supplies and stationery in general, as well as online consultations and appointments.

The company was founded in 2023 by Marcos Macho, whose love for design and compassion for others led him to create this company to offer creative, affordable and fast solutions in various digital and physical services.
